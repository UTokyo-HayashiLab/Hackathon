Generative AI Hackathon: Research & Education Project

This project aims to address societal challenges through AI and natural language processing technologies and ideas, while also supporting student education.
Participants from various organizations collaborate to exchange innovative ideas and rapidly transform them into tangible outcomes.
The research results from the project have led to significant achievements, including acceptance at top-tier international conferences.
