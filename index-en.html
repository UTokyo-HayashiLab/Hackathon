Generative AI Hackathon: Research & Education Project

This project aims to address societal challenges through technologies and ideas related to AI and natural language processing and to support student education.
Participants from various organizations come together to exchange innovative ideas and transform them into tangible results within a short period.
The research outcomes from the project have led to significant achievements, such as acceptance at top-tier international conferences.
